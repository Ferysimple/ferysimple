03 — STORY

A Brand Born of Soil.

從土地長出來的品牌

What We Believe

On the western edge of the Pacific lies an island embraced by the sea. Within it — food makers turn island botanicals into jellies that nourish tired eyes. Fragrance formulators craft scents safe enough for cats to share the room. Bakers guard 1962 recipes that have never changed.

These stories deserve to be heard by the world.

What we do is not export trade. It is walking — into every kitchen, every laboratory, every workshop in Taiwan — on behalf of buyers around the world, carrying back what is truly the best.

我們相信的事

太平洋的西岸，有一座被海洋環抱的島嶼。在這裡，食品師傅把島上的植物，做成能修復雙眼疲勞的果凍；香氛配方師調出連貓咪都能共處一室的氣味；烘焙職人守著一份 1962 年的食譜，五十年沒改過。

這些故事，世界值得聽見。

我們做的不是出口貿易。而是替全世界的買家，走進台灣每一間廚房、每一座實驗室、每一處作坊，把最好的那一份帶回去。
